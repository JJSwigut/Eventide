Eventide 2.04 adds clear government-data attribution. Settings now provides direct links to NOAA Tides & Currents, the National Weather Service, the National Data Buoy Center, NOAA nowCOAST, and NOAA/NWS map services. The app and Play listing also clearly state that Eventide is independent and does not represent, receive endorsement from, or have an affiliation with any government entity.
