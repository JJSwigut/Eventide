Plan your time on the water with a clear view of the coast.

Eventide brings tide predictions, marine observations, coastal weather, and map overlays together in one focused app. Browse nearby NOAA stations on the map, open a station for a readable tide chart, and see the conditions that matter before you head out.

TIDES AT A GLANCE
- Read daily high and low tide times and heights
- Follow the tide curve and current position
- Browse upcoming days without leaving the station

WEATHER AND MARINE CONDITIONS
- View National Weather Service forecasts
- Check nearby National Data Buoy Center observations, including wind, waves, and water temperature
- See observation time, freshness, source, and buoy distance

COASTAL MAP TOOLS
- Explore NOAA tide stations on an interactive map
- Toggle radar, weather, and cloud overlays
- Return quickly to your location

YOUR STATIONS, YOUR UNITS
- Favorite stations and choose a home station
- Set alerts before selected high or low tides
- Choose feet or meters, Fahrenheit or Celsius, and 12- or 24-hour time

Eventide uses data from NOAA, the National Weather Service, and the National Data Buoy Center. Conditions can change quickly; always consult official navigation and safety information before making decisions on the water.
